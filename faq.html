Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for new and returning Minecherry players.

What is Mine Cherry Network?

Mine Cherry Network is a fun and competitive Minecraft server offering unique gameplay, custom features, and an active community.

How do I join the server?

Open Minecraft Java Edition, go to Multiplayer, click Add Server, enter the IP address, then join and start playing.

Where can I get the server IP?

You can find the server IP in our Discord server: https://discord.gg/ZP4bpFRrsg

What are the server rules?

Basic rules include no hacking or cheating, no spamming or advertising, respecting all players and staff, and no abusive language. Breaking rules may result in punishment.

What ranks are available?

We currently offer Admin — 300 PKR, Lord — 700 PKR (Coming Soon), and King — 1000 PKR (Coming Soon).

How do I purchase a rank?

Join our Discord and contact staff or follow the instructions in the store or payment section.

I got scammed or lost items. What do I do?

Report it immediately in the Discord server with proof like screenshots or recordings. Staff will review your case.

How can I become staff?

Fill out the staff application form on the website or Discord, stay active, and make sure you follow all rules.

How do I report bugs?

You can report bugs in the Discord server under the #bug-reports channel.

Is the server 24/7 online?

We try our best to keep the server online 24/7, but occasional downtime may happen for maintenance or updates.

Do you have a YouTube channel?

Yes. Check it out here: YouTube Channel

Can I invite my friends?

Of course. The more players, the better. Invite your friends and help grow the community.
