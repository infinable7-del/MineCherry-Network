Server Rules

Read the official Minecherry rules before playing.

These rules are here to keep the server fair, respectful, and fun for everyone.

General Behaviour

  • Be respectful to all players
  • No bullying, harassment, or hate speech
  • No spamming or flooding chat
  • No trolling or intentionally annoying players
  • Follow all staff instructions
  • Use appropriate language
  • Report rule breakers instead of arguing
  • Do not create drama or conflicts
  • Use common sense at all times
  • Staff decisions are final

Chat & Communication

  • Keep chat clean and friendly
  • No advertising other servers
  • No offensive or inappropriate language
  • No impersonation of players or staff
  • Avoid excessive caps or spam
  • Stay on topic in chats
  • No leaking private messages
  • Respect all players in conversations
  • Use allowed languages only
  • No toxic arguments

Gameplay & Fair Play

  • No hacks, cheats, or unfair mods
  • No exploiting bugs or glitches
  • No duping or item abuse
  • No lag machines or crash attempts
  • Do not bypass anti-cheat systems
  • Play fairly at all times
  • Respect game modes and mechanics
  • No stat boosting or fake grinding
  • Report bugs instead of abusing
  • No unfair advantages

Building & Griefing

  • No griefing or destroying builds
  • No stealing from other players
  • Respect land claims and bases
  • No trap builds meant to harm others
  • No TNT/lava abuse in protected areas
  • Keep builds appropriate
  • Do not block paths or important areas
  • Avoid lag-heavy redstone builds
  • Ask before building near others
  • Staff can remove harmful builds

Economy & Trading

  • No scamming in trades
  • Use fair pricing in shops
  • No exploiting shop glitches
  • No illegal or duped items
  • No real-money trading (RMT)
  • Respect crate and reward systems
  • No economy abuse
  • Follow shop rules
  • Report scams immediately
  • Staff may reset abused economy

Teleport & Trapping

  • No TP trapping (lava, void, mobs, etc.)
  • No fake TPA requests
  • No teleport kill setups
  • No trap bases for teleport kills
  • No abuse of /home, /warp, /spawn
  • No repeated trap killing (loop trapping)
  • No TP scams
  • PvP must be fair and known
  • Use teleport for fair gameplay only
  • Staff decides what counts as trapping

Events & Tournaments

  • Follow all event rules
  • Be on time for events
  • No cheating or unfair teaming
  • Listen to event hosts
  • Stay active during participation
  • Respect all players
  • No toxicity during events
  • Follow prize rules
  • Report issues instantly
  • Staff decisions are final

Ranks & Perks

  • Do not abuse rank permissions
  • No unfair advantage abuse
  • Respect all ranks
  • No sharing accounts
  • Purchases are final
  • No fake giveaways
  • Payments must be legit
  • Protect your account
  • Staff must act responsibly
  • Abuse = rank removal

Punishment & Enforcement

  • Breaking rules leads to punishment
  • Repeated offenses = harsher penalties
  • No ban evasion
  • Punishments include mute, kick, ban
  • Evidence required for reports
  • Appeals allowed (if system exists)
  • No loophole abuse
  • Temporary or permanent bans apply
  • Severe violations = instant ban
  • Staff decisions are final
